About

Discover the Hidden Beauty of Maydolong, Eastern Samar

Deep within the lush forests of Maydolong, Eastern Samar lies one of the island's most refreshing secrets: Binubuwakan Cold Spring. Managed by the Municipal Tourism Office, this nature retreat promises an unforgettable experience for visitors. Whether you're here to bask in the cool, clear waters or to embrace the serenity of the forest, Binubuwakan Cold Spring offers a perfect escape from the busy world.

Immerse yourself in the beauty of the surrounding forest and explore scenic trails that lead you to the pristine cold waters of the spring. The peaceful atmosphere and the natural surroundings make it the ideal spot for relaxation, reflection, and adventure. Perfect for family outings, solo retreats, or a fun day with friends, Binubuwakan Cold Spring offers something for everyone.
